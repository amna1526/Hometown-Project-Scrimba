Visit Lahore, Pakistan

You are very welcome in the city of lively hearted people!

Some must visit spots in Lahore:

Baadshaahi Mosque

This majestic mosque is an excellent artifact of Mughal architecture.

Punjab University

Pay a visit to the ancient educational institute of Pakistan and enjoy its aesthetic beauty!

Parks

When tired, go to any one of the many beautiful parks in Lahore!

Minar-e-Pakistan

Minar-e-Pakistan is the historical place where the Pakistan Resolution was signed. It is a sign of our freedom!
